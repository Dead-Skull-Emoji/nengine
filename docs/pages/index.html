The simplstic hobbyist engine.

Nengine is an open-sourced game engine made by an organization of hobbyist programmers called the Li Institution.

It is made for simplicity, being easier to use than most other game engines. It is written in Rust, with support for writing games in C#, Python, and JavaScript.
